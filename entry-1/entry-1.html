ingredients

  • 1/3 heavy cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cupcake flour
  • 1 tbsp active dry yeast
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 and 1/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 2/3 cup superfine sugar
  • 1/4 cup vegetable shortening
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1/8 teaspoon vanila extract

pineapple bun

instructions

  1. Add the heavy cream, milk, egg, sugar, cake flour, bread flour, yeast, and salt (in that order). Mix for 15 minutes.
  2. Cover the bowl with a damp towel and place in a warm spot for 1 hour.
  3. Stir dough for another 5 minutes to get rid of air bubbles. Place dough on a lightly floured surface and cut into 12 equal pieces. Form circular buns and place on a baking sheet.
  4. Cover the buns with a clean, dry kitchen towel and let rise for another hour.
  5. For the topping, add the dry milk powder to a mixing bowl.Add the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and superfine sugar. Stir to combine. Add the shortening, milk, egg yolk, and vanilla.
  6. Mix everything together into a dough. Cover tightly with plastic wrap and set aside at room temperature.
  7. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Separate the topping dough into 12 equal pieces and roll them into balls. On a clean surface, roll out each ball into a flat circle and place one onto each bun.
  8. Brush with egg yolk and bake at 350 degrees for 12-13 minutes.
