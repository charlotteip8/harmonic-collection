ingredients

  • 4 slices of thick bread
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • butter
  • Condensed milk
  • Maple syrup or honey
  • Peanut butter

HK style french toast

instructions

  1. Trim the crusts off the bread, then sandwich two slices together with a bit of butter or peanut butter in between
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium heat and add butter. Dip each sandwich in the egg mixture, making sure to coat both sides well.
  4. Place the coated bread in the skillet and fry until golden brown on both sides, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  5. Once cooked, remove from the skillet and drizzle with condensed milk. You can also add maple syrup, honey, or butter if you like.
