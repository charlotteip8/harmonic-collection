ingredients

  • 1 package (about 400g) of silken tofu
  • 2 tablespoons agar-agar powder
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup rock sugar (or regular sugar)
  • 1 cup water (save for sugar syrup)
  • 1-2 slices ginger

Tofu Pudding (Dou Fu Fa)

instructions

  1. In a small saucepan, dissolve the agar-agar powder in 1/2 cup water. Bring to a simmer and stir for about 2-3 minutes until dissolved.
  2. In a blender or food processor, blend the silken tofu until smooth and creamy.
  3. Once the agar mixture has cooled slightly, slowly add it to the tofu puree, blend until completely mixed.
  4. Pour the tofu mixture into small bowls or serving dishes. Let it cool at room temperature for about 10-15 minutes, then refrigerate for 1-2 hours to set.
  5. In a small saucepan, combine the rock sugar, water, and ginger slices. Bring it to a boil, stirring occasionally to dissolve the sugar.
  6. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 5-10 minutes until the syrup thickens slightly.
  7. After the syrup has simmered, strain out the ginger slices and allow the syrup to cool to room temperature.
