ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil (plus more for the pan)

Egg Waffle (Gei Dan Zai)

instructions

  1. Preheat your egg waffle pan over medium heat and lightly grease it with vegetable oil.
  2. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, cornstarch, sugar, baking powder, and salt until well combined.
  3. In another bowl, whisk the eggs. Then add the milk, water, vanilla extract, and vegetable oil. Mix until smooth.
  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, whisking until you have a smooth batter. Let it rest for about 10-15 minutes.
  5. Pour enough batter into the preheated waffle pan to fill it about halfway. Close the pan and cook for about 2-3 minutes, flipping it halfway through, until the waffles are golden brown and crispy.
  6. Carefully remove the egg waffles from the pan and serve immediately. You can enjoy them plain or top with fresh fruit, ice cream, chocolate sauce, or condensed milk.
