ingredients

* for plain rice rolls *

  • Rice flour
  • Corn starch
  • Tapioca starch
  • Wheat starch
  • Water

* toppings & sauces *

  • Hoisin sauce
  • Soy sauce
  • Peanut sauce
  • Hot sauce
  • Sesame seeds

rice noodle rolls

instructions

  1. Put 1 cup of rice flour, 1 teaspoon of tapioca starch, 1 teaspoon of wheat starch and 1 teaspoon of cornstarch into a bowl.
  2. Pour 2 cups & 3 teaspoons of water into the bowl. Mix and stir it well.
  3. Brush some vegetable oil on a tray and pour some rice flour mixture from step 2 into the tray.
  4. Steam it for 2 minutes after water is boiling. Repeat steps 2-4 a few times.
  5. Put Â½ tablespoon of peanut butter in a container and add 1 tablespoon of water. Mix it very well until peanut butter is a little bit liquid.
  6. When the rice flour is done, let it cool down a little bit. Then, use a spatula to scrape the side. And, roll the rice noodle sheet up.
  7. Repeat steps 7 and 8 a few times. Use the spatula to cut rice noodle rolls into pieces.
  8. Add 1/2 tablespoon of sesame oil, 1/2 tablespoon of soy sauce, 1.5 tablespoons of hoisin sauce, some peanut sauce and sprinkle sesame seeds. (adjust the amount of sauce to your taste.)
